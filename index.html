About Me

I am a Multidisciplinary Designer who enjoys using various tools to solve a given problem and also using other facets of the creative industry, from Graphic Design, UI/UX Design & Accessibity.

My work as a Freelancer and working with start-ups has afforded me the privilege and skills of laying a foundation and creating a clear path for the products that I create.

Having an understanding that each product has its own unique approach is key to solving a given problem. Special care and attention has to be taken into consideration in the development stage.

The current tech has afforded me the opportunity to visiually tell my story, and better understand Users by employing a user-centred approach to problem solving on everyday problems.

What I Offer

My passion and goal is to help you make your business standout.

Brand Identity

The way Users relate and identify who you are is key to a thriving business. It is what helps Users distinguish what you stand for from the rest of the businesses in the market. Through visual elements like the logo this objective can be realised where you can tell a story that is unique to you.

Photography

A combination of Graphics and Photography is the most potent way of telling a story. It makes it easier for the audience to comprehend your mission. My process involves blending the two to create powerful visuals (profiles, posters,etc) that can be easily embraced by the Audience.

Web Design

Your online Presents is paromount to your visibility. Incorporating design and tech helps to create stunning websites, and flexible layouts that adapt to any device that you might be using.

Product Design

Getting the right product approach will definitely go a long way in creating a product that is easily accepted by users. Through various deliverables I offer a clear cut product strategy that will ensure that you offer users what they need

UX Design

Understanding how Users experience your products and services helps to make them better and more relatable. This also helps to improve or come up with better User Interfaces that help to explore the products easily.

Mobile UI Design

The success of any Mobile application is determined by what Users see and in particular the User Interface (UI). Through using various UX deliverables I create smooth and easy to navigate UI that will help you start and finish tasks with ease.
